About Lesion Network Mapping

Lesion Network Mapping

Browser-based lesion atlas overlap for neuroimaging workflows.

This app loads a structural T1 (or a manual MNI-aligned lesion mask) locally, runs the full lesion-network-mapping chain in the browser, and never uploads patient data.

The pipeline: SynthStrip brain extraction → SynthStroke baseline lesion segmentation → SynthMorph deformable registration to MNI152 (1603 1mm) → affine-aware resample to the Yeo7 grid (99×117×95 2mm) → Yeo7 parcel overlap → Yeo7 group-FC weighted-sum (ADHD-200 N=30) → threshold + cluster cleanup. All stages are exposed as individual buttons; "Run full pipeline" chains them in one click.

Privacy

Your data remains on your device.

Uploaded images and masks are read into browser memory for local viewing and analysis.

What this means

  • No patient image data, lesion masks, voxel values, screenshots, or generated outputs are uploaded by the app.
  • Atlas and model assets may be fetched by the browser when a workflow needs them.
  • Downloaded result files are created locally from browser memory.

Third-party services

Static app files and public atlas assets may be served from external hosting, but patient-derived files are not sent to those services by the analysis code.
